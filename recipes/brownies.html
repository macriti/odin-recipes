Brownies

Description

This is the best brownie recipe you can ever get. Make sure to get all ingredients before starting the preparation

  • Eggs x 2
  • Powdered sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract
  1. Mix dry and wet ingredients in separate bowls
  2. combine wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour it in 8x8 baking pan
  4. Bake on 180 Celcius
